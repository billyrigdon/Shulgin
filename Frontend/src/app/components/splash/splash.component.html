Hello and welcome to Shulgin! This is my passion project and I thought I'd give a little background on why this app exists in the first place. I struggle with ADHD and Bipolar disorder and have to take an assortment of prescription drugs to keep myself under control.

There was a lot of trial and error figuring out which drugs worked best for me and I found that there was not a lot of information regarding the drugs that I was on and how they reacted with each other and decided to create something to help people who might have similar issues.

Shulgin is an app for tracking your mood while taking pharmaceuticals and sharing your mood tracking data anonymously. As you track your mood and create journal entries, they are stripped of user information and saved as stories that are searchable by other users.

Although you can opt out of public stories, it is recommended to share your experiences so that other users are more able to identify various effects and interactions between different drugs.
